3 · Receptionist setup checklist

  • List your top 10 caller questions and the exact answers (hours, pricing rules, service area).
  • Decide what the receptionist should do: book, qualify, or route to you.
  • Set your urgent-call rule (what gets forwarded to a human, and when).
  • Connect your calendar or booking tool.
  • Write the call summary text you want after every call.
  • Test with 10 real-world call scenarios before going live.
  • Put the number on your site, Google profile, and ads.
